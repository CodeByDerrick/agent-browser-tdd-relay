# Review Checklist

Relative path: `automation/checklists/review-checklist.md`
Project: Agent Bridge

Purpose: This checklist defines the compact review expectations for slice, checkpoint, and milestone outputs in Agent Bridge. Use it to confirm that work solved the intended problem, evidence is credible, and the next-step material stays appropriately small and actionable.

- Confirm the slice solved the stated brief
- Confirm evidence matches success criteria
- Confirm verification actually happened
- Confirm the next brief is minimal
- Confirm risks are called out without bloating context
